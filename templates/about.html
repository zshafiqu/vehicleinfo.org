About Me!

Hello there, thanks for checking out my project! My name is Zain Shafique, and I am the founder and creator of vehicleinfo.org (also known as the Vehicle Information Project).

I am currently a fourth year student at the University of California, Santa Cruz where I study computer science. Motivation for this project came from the fact that I am a HUGE car enthusiast, and me trying to find accessible APIs online that would allow me to access information about cars.

Searching online gave me no results for open source or public APIs offering vehicle data, as most of them had been commercialized and only available B2B. I found a need, and decided to develop this web application. I spent a few days aggregating the data and storing it in CSV files that I eventually pushed to a database. Following this, I built out a Python app to serve the data through a web interface and an API. This project will always be public and open source, and I will continue to add to it. If anyone is interested in contributing, the source code is published via GitHub and I am always happy to help get you set up!
